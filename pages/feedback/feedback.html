[electroacoustic effect]

Refers to a sonic loop wherein a sound that is produced can reverberate on the origin of the sound, such as the larsen effect in an electric guitar. The guitarist can orient the guitar in relation to the amplifier to allow to strings to reverberate on the sound produced by the amplifier.
