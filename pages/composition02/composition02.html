Composition 02
—

Texture

Refers to the constant state of transition to which a sound ambience is subject. The relationships between different sonic elements are constantly fluctuating. Furthermore, our subjective perception of these relationships changes. This term is derived from the greek “Metablolos” and the pre-Socratic philosopher Heraclitus.
