Distortion
—

[elementary effect]

Manipulation of the spectral envelope of a sound sequence. This can be seen electroacoustically in a voluntary or involuntary manner. Electrostatic fuzz can accidentally be produced during the amplification or recording of instruments. Distortion can also be produced voluntarily in order to change the colouring of an instrument for a different emotional effect, as in the case of electric guitar.
